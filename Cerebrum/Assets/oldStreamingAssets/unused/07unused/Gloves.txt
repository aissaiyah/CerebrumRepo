#Gloves - Check to put on Gloves

$Gloves align ZoomSpot
ZoomSpot Menu.Question 'Put on Gloves?'
ZoomSpot Menu.on ZoomSpot
WaitFor
	ZoomSpot Menu.Done
Wait 1
If
	ZoomSpot menu.result $Yes
Then
	ScoreTracker taskCorrect 11 true
Else
	ScoreTracker taskCorrect 11 false
Endif
$Gloves reset
$Gloves align $GlovesPos
Return
