ChamberCheck off
ChangeDripSpeed on
ChangeDripSpeed/Chamber/DripRate setsaline 0
SoundPlayer playsound 4
Label 'Loop'
If
	ChangeDripSpeed/Chamber/Increase pressed
Then
	ChangeDripSpeed/Chamber/Increase reset
	ChangeDripSpeed/Chamber/DripRate raisesaline
	SoundPlayer raiseinterval
Endif
If
	ChangeDripSpeed/Chamber/Decrease pressed
Then
	ChangeDripSpeed/Chamber/Decrease reset
	ChangeDripSpeed/Chamber/DripRate reducesaline
Endif
If
	ChangeDripSpeed/Chamber/DripRate salineamount 3
Then
	Wait 2
	SoundPlayer stopsound
	ScoreTracker gain 1
	ScoreTracker complete 5
	Do '07/FourHourStart.txt'
Else
	Goto 'Loop'
Endif
